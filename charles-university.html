EdUmatics Partner - Charles University, Prague, Faculty of Education

Charles University, Prague, Faculty of Education

The Institution

Charles University in Prague was founded in 1348 and is one of the world's oldest universities. The Faculty of Education is one of seventeen faculties associated under the umbrella of the Charles University in Prague. Its primary goal is to train teachers and other pedagogical personnel for all types of schools.
