Lehrstuhl für Didaktik der Mathematik, Universität Würzburg

The Institution

For many years, the research focus of the Department for Didactics of Mathematics has been on the use of new technologies. This includes the development and conduction of online courses for teacher training, the evaluation of the use of handheld devices and software like dynamic geometry or computer algebra systems.
