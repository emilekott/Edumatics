Davison CE High School and Technology College for Girls

The Institution

Davison CE High School and Technology Schools for Girls is a schools for students aged 12-16 and it was first designated as a Technology College in 1999. It was originally founded in 1812 to serve the poor children of Worthing. The mathematics department is renowned for its innivatve practice involving the use of technology across the school.
